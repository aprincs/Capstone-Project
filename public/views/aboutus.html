About Us

Our History

The earliest known example of cars being offered for rent dates to 1906.

Our agency is a company that rents automobiles for short periods of time, generally ranging from a few hours to a few weeks. We have numerous local branches (which allow a user to return a vehicle to a different location), we are located near Riga airport and near city center, also our website allowing online reservations.

Easy Car Rent is an ongoing Latvian success story. Our guiding principles, and humble beginning, revolve around personal honesty and integrity. We believe in strengthening our communities one neighborhood at a time, serving our customers as if they were our family, and rewarding hard work. These things are as true today as they were when we were founded in 2004.

Today, our developed network means Easy Car Rent is the largest transportation solutions provider in Latvia. We offer car and vans rentals, as well as car sharing.
